Huge-IT Google Maps. One more perfect tool from Huge-IT. Improved Google Maps, where  we have our special contribution. Most simple and effective tool for rapid creation of individual Google Maps in posts and pages.

With our Google s, you can provide your visitors with information about the exact location of your company/place. To make sure that people can find your place, you should add the appropriate information to the markers of Google maps. With the help of this plugin give information on the right location of your business, you distribute information about yourself, get free traffic and increases the number of transitions to your page from the usual search on Google.
On the maps you can create marker with has many beautiful styles. besides given styles you also can upload your preferable image and set it instead of marker
We also added useful special features. You can select the exact location, track or spread area of a place.

Polygons - bounded highlighted area on the maps, showing the specific range, limited with geometric figure. You can easy create a polygon, give shape and set styles on it. With polygon you can show to your visitors the borders of a specific location. 

Polylines - continuous line composed of one or more line segments, which creates specific track. With Polylines you can highlight specific road/track, give styles to the path and change it whenever you need. Show to you visitors how to get to your place and many other things
Circles - round area, showing the specific range. With circle you can take the area into round border and give styles to it.
All these features can quickly and easily adapted to your requirements and needs. 

Google Maps can be easily integrated with the site. On Web sites of WordPress can be easily inserted, customized and used in posts, pages or as a simple widget. And your visitors can  instantly understand how to get to you, you just need to provide the coordinates. 
We made for you a big list of customization. Most of them are absolutely free and easy to use.

You only need to install the plugin from WordPress, and see all options on admin page, with demonstration map, next to it, so you can watch all your changes before inserting into post/page.

== Installation ==

### First download the ZIP file - Google Maps ,

1. Log in to your website administrator panel.   
2. Go to Plugins page, and add new plugin.    
3. Upload [WordPress Google Maps](http://wordpress.org/plugins/google-maps). 
4. Click `Install Now` button.     
5. Then click `Activate Plugin` button. 

Now you can set your Image Gallery options, images and use our Google Maps.

==Step 1. Adding a Google Maps==

**Step 1 Add Google Maps in post/page**

In order to add created Google Maps plugin in post/page, copy/past the shortcode. Or add like a widget from widgets page. 

**Step 2 Edit Google Maps**

2.1 Google Maps Options. 

Google Maps names. Give a names to your Google maps

Enable Pan-Controller. Select to enable movement controller - Google Maps

Enable Zoom-Controller. Select to enable zoom controller - Google Maps

Enable Map-Type-Controller. Enables map controller in order to view satellite as well - Google Maps

Enable Scale-Controller. Select to show scale - Google Maps

Enable Street-View-Controller. Select to be able to see streets with “little man” - Google Maps

Enable Overview-Map-Controller. Select to see map controller on the bottom right corner - Google Maps

Default Zoom. Set the zoom level for maps - Google Maps

Center Address. Give address to maps center - Google Maps

Center Latitude. Set latitude for the maps center - Google Maps

Center Longitude. Set longitude for maps center - Google Maps

Map width. Give size for maps width (in presents) - Google Maps

Map height. Give size for maps height (in px) - Google Maps

Map align. Put your map on the left, right or center within  theme container - Google Maps

Border Radius. Set the radius for maps borders - Google Maps

PRO Map type. Choose one from 4 different views of the maps - Google Maps

PRO Marker Infowindow Open On. Choose how to open information on marker - Google Maps

**2.2 Markers - Google Maps**

Click +Add New Marker and create unlimited markers for your specific places - Google Maps
.
Right Click on map wherever you want marker to appear. Hold pressed and change marker’s position moving mouse - Google Maps

Address. Give address to the marker - Google Maps

Latitude. Select latitude to the  marker’s place - Google Maps

Longitude. Select longitude to the marker’s place - Google Maps

Animation. Choose animation for the marker - Google Maps

Title. Give title to the marker - Google Maps

Description. Give title to the marker/place - Google Maps

PRO Choose Marker Icon from our list of stylish markers - Google Maps

PRO Size of Icon. Choose marker size - Google Maps

PRO Custom Marker Icon. You can upload your preferable icon(picture) for marker - Google Maps

**2.3 Polygons - Google Maps**

Polygons - bounded highlighted area on the map, showing the specific range, limited with geometric figure - Google Maps

Click +Add New Polygons and create new one for your specific area - Google Maps

Right click and hold to set polygon’s boundaries - Google Maps

Name. Give name to the Polygon - Google Maps

Link. Add link with external URL, which will take to another page - Google Maps

Latitude & Longitude Data. Manually change or add new borders of polygon - Google Maps

Line Transparency. Select the level of transparency for boundary line - Google Maps

Line Color. Choose color for  boundary line - Google Maps

Line Width. Set width for  boundary line - Google Maps

Fill Transparency. Select the level of transparency for internal area of polygon - Google Maps

Fill Color. Choose color  for internal area of polygon - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Fill Transparency. Select the level of transparency for internal area of polygon while hover - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Fill Color. Choose color  for internal area of polygon while hover - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Line Transparency. Select the level of transparency for boundary line while hover - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Line Color. Choose color for  boundary line while hover - Google Maps

**2.4 Polylines - Google Maps**

Polyline - continuous line composed of one or more line segments, which creates specific track - Google Maps

Name. Give name to the Polyline - Google Maps

Latitude & Longitude Data. Right click and select latitude & longitude data for Polyline - Google Maps

Line Transparency. Select the level of transparency of  Polyline - Google Maps

Line Color. Choose color  of  Polyline - Google Maps

Line Width. Set width  of  Polyline - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Line Color. Choose color  of  Polyline while hover - Google Maps

PRO On-Hover Line Transparency.  Select the level of Polyline transparency while hover - Google Maps
